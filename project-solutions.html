How We Deliver

A delivery model built around your outcome

Every project starts with the result you need — then we shape the team, timeline and process around it.

Fixed-Scope Projects

A defined outcome, on a defined timeline

For well-scoped builds — new platforms, migrations, integrations — we agree the scope, timeline and cost upfront, then deliver against it with full transparency.

  • Clear milestones & fixed-price billing
  • Dedicated PM & QA throughout
  • Best for greenfield builds & migrations
Agile Squads

A dedicated squad, working in sprints

For evolving products and roadmaps, we embed a cross-functional squad — dev, QA, design — that works in sprints alongside your product team.

  • Sprint-based delivery with regular demos
  • Backlog managed jointly with your team
  • Best for ongoing product development
Managed Support & AMS

Keep what you've built running and improving

Once a project is live, our application management services keep it stable, secure and evolving — with SLAs that match your business needs.

  • L1/L2/L3 support with defined SLAs
  • Continuous improvement & small enhancements
  • Best for post-launch stability & growth
How It Works

From idea to launch — a journey we manage together

A transparent, step-by-step process designed to reduce risk and keep you in control at every stage.

01

Discovery & Scoping

We understand your goals, users and constraints, then define scope, architecture and a realistic timeline.

02

Design & Architecture

UX/UI design and technical architecture are finalized and validated with your stakeholders before development begins.

03

Build & Iterate

Development proceeds in iterations, with regular demos so you can see progress and provide feedback early.

04

Test & Launch

Thorough QA, performance and security testing precede a managed go-live, with support on standby.

05

Support & Grow

Post-launch, we keep the platform stable and help you plan and build the next phase of the roadmap.
