Life with AIT

What it's really like to work at Adhiran Infotech

From team outings across our global offices to the benefits that support our people day to day — here's a look inside our culture.

Benefits

What you get as part of the AIT team

Wherever you're based — India, USA, UAE, Singapore, Japan or Australia — our benefits are designed to support your work and your life.

Health & Wellness Cover

Comprehensive medical insurance for employees and dependents, plus access to wellness programs.

Flexible Working

Hybrid and remote options across most roles, with flexibility to work across time zones.

Learning & Certifications

Sponsored certifications across AWS, Azure, Salesforce, SAP and more — plus internal learning programs.

Performance Bonuses

Transparent performance reviews tied to bonuses and clear paths for career progression.

Global Mobility

Opportunities to work on projects across our India, US, UAE, Singapore, Japan and Australia offices.

Paid Time Off

Generous annual leave, public holidays by location, and parental leave policies.

Referral Rewards

Earn rewards for referring great talent into roles across any of our offices.

Modern Tools & Tech

Work with the latest cloud, AI and collaboration tools — no outdated systems holding you back.
