Team Models

Three ways to scale your team with us

Whether you need a project pod, a dedicated offshore center, or ongoing talent supply across markets — we structure the model around how you want to operate.

Team Hiring & Pods

A cross-functional team — developers, QA, designers, PM — hired and managed as a single unit for your project or product.

  • Single point of contact for the whole team
  • Scales up or down with your roadmap
  • Best for ongoing product development

Offshore Development Center (ODC)

A dedicated, branded extension of your company — your own team, infrastructure and processes, fully managed by us.

  • Dedicated facility, infrastructure & team
  • HR, admin, IT & compliance handled for you
  • Best for long-term, large-scale capacity

Onsite & Offshore Talent Supply

A blended model — local consultants placed onsite with your team, supported by an offshore bench for delivery and surge capacity.

  • Local presence with offshore cost efficiency
  • Active in Singapore, Dubai, Japan & Australia
  • Best for client-facing & regulated roles
Offshore Development Center

Your own team, without the overhead of setting one up

An ODC gives you a dedicated team that works exclusively for you — under your processes, security standards and reporting lines — without the cost and complexity of setting up a legal entity, office and HR function yourself.

We handle recruitment, infrastructure, HR, payroll, compliance and facilities — so your leadership can focus on what the team delivers, not how it's run.

Delivery Models

Onsite, offshore, or a blend of both

Different roles and projects call for different presence. We help you design the right mix.

Onsite Talent

Local presence, embedded with your team
  • Consultants placed directly at your office or client site
  • Ideal for client-facing, regulated or on-call roles
  • Local compliance, payroll and visa support handled

Offshore Talent

Delivery teams based in our global centers
  • Cost-efficient delivery capacity for development & support
  • Time-zone overlap managed for daily collaboration
  • Easily scales as a blended extension of an onsite team

How We Work

From requirement to a fully running team

Whether it's a pod, an ODC, or ongoing talent supply — the process starts with understanding how you want the team to operate.

01

Define the Model

We discuss your goals — project pod, ODC, or onsite/offshore supply — and agree on team size, roles and location mix.

02

Source & Onboard

We recruit and onboard the team, setting up infrastructure, access and processes as needed for the chosen model.

03

Operate & Integrate

The team works as an extension of yours — with reporting, communication and tools aligned to how you operate.

04

Scale Over Time

As needs grow, we add roles, locations or shift toward a full ODC — without disrupting what's already working.
