Automation in Action

What industrial automation looks like in practice

A look at how connected systems, automation and AI come together on the plant floor.

Predictive Maintenance

From reactive repairs to predictive action

Sensors on critical equipment feed vibration, temperature and performance data into ML models that flag developing issues — so maintenance happens on a schedule, not in an emergency.

  • Condition-based monitoring across critical assets
  • Early-warning alerts to maintenance teams
  • Reduced unplanned downtime by up to 60%
Quality Inspection

Catching defects the human eye misses

Vision systems trained to detect specific defect types run inline, flagging issues in real time and feeding data back into process improvement — without slowing the line down.

  • Real-time defect detection on the production line
  • Reduced scrap and rework rates
  • Data feeds continuous process improvement
Operations Visibility

One view, across every plant

Real-time OEE, downtime and throughput dashboards bring multi-plant operations into a single view — so leadership and plant teams work from the same data.

  • Live OEE, downtime & throughput tracking
  • Cross-plant benchmarking & reporting
